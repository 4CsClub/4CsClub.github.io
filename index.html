About Us

We are a small, friendly club that welcomes anyone who wishes to rally with us, whether you have a caravan, motor home, trailer tent, or tent. Our weekend rallies usually run from midday on a Thursday to Sunday afternoon, depending on the rally's location and the marshal in charge. You can arrive on any day you wish. We also organize holiday rallies that typically last one or two weeks, sometimes longer.

Our Committee

Air Ambulance

Throughout the year on our rallies we aim to generate funds for The Derbyshire, Leicestershire & Rutland Air Ambulance, our chosen charity. Over previous years we have generated on average over £1000 to the charity. It was decided by a vote at the 2022 AGM that the Derbyshire, Leicestershire & Rutland Air Ambulance will be the permanent charity that the club supports going forward.
